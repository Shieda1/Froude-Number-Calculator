What is the froude number?
The froude number is a dimensionless number and basicaly it is the ratio of the flow inertia to the external field.
How to calculate froude number?
To calculate froude number you should use the following formula:
Fr = v / (gd)^1/2
where:
Fr = froude number
d = debth of flow
g = gravitational acceleration = 9.81 m/s^2
v = celerity of a small surface wave
Why use froude number?
The froude number is used in order to delineate the boundry between laminar and turbulent flow conditions in open channels.
